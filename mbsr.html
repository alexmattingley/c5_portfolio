MBSR Mentorship

When Allan originally came to us with the idea to create a website that encompased what he did as a MBSR teacher Lucy and I immediately started working on developing a simple beautiful useable wordpress site. Although there is information on the site, its primary purpose is to convey a feeling hence the focus on images and short readable blocks of text.

Lucy did the lions share of the photography and image collection for the site. We worked together to design the right look, feel and user experience and then I took up the torch and did the development side of things. I am quite proud of the way it turned out and I hope you can enjoy it as well.
